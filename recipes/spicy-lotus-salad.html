Spicy Cucumber Salad

Description

The beautiful crunch of the lotus roots amongst the myriad of flavors of spicy, fresh, savory and scents from the fragrant aromatics. This is delicious as a side dish with rice or snack (I just take bitefuls of this between meetings)

Ingredients

  • Lotus Root
  • Cilantro
  • Fried Peanuts
  • Green Onions
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Sesame Oil
  • Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce
  • MSG
  • Chili Oil

Steps

  1. Wash all the veggies: garlic, lotus roots, cilantro, green onion. Cube the peeled lotus roots.
  2. Mix the condiments in a bowl
  3. Add in cilantro, fried peanuts, green onion, garlic to the bowl of lotus roots.
  4. Mix the veggies and the sauce together and enjoy!
