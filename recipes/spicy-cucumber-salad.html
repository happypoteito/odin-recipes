Spicy Cucumber Salad

Description

An addictive combination of refreshing cucumber with a spicy in-your-face dressing balanced with a generous handful of green onion and eschallots.

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Cilantro
  • Fried Peanuts
  • Green Onions
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Sesame Oil
  • Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce
  • MSG
  • Chili Oil

Steps

  1. Wash all the veggies: garlic, cucumbers, cilantro, green onion. Cube the cucumbers
  2. Mix some salt on the cucumbers to flavor it and also absorb some of the water out to maintain a crisp flavor
  3. While waiting for the cucumber to sweat, mix the condiments in a bowl
  4. Remove the extra water from cucumber, and gently wash away the salt. Add in cilantro, fried peanuts, green onion, garlic
  5. Mix the veggies and the sauce together and enjoy!
