Pork Chop with Rice Cake

Description

Pork Chop Rice Cake is a special delight, widely consumed in Shanghai, that also happens to be quite economical. It boasts a long history, measured to almost 50 years. This snack is commonly prepared by the method of frying, it usually using such ingredients as a large pork chop and rice cakes. The preparation of this dish calls for the chop to be fried on both sides over medium heat until it reaches a golden brown color, along with a piece of rice cake. This process does not require a lot of time, so that the dish could preserve both: the savory taste of the pork chop and the crispy texture of the rice cake.

Ingredients

  • A bottle of Coca-cola
  • Rice Cakes
  • Pork Chops
  • Dried Bay Leaf
  • Chinese Cinnamon
  • Star Anise
  • Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Sesame
  • Chinese Cooking Wine
  • Ginger
  • Scallion

Steps

  1. Chop the pork chop in pieces
  2. Filled the stock pot with cold water, add in a spoon of Chinese Cooking Wine, along with couple slice of ginger and scallion.
  3. Put the pork chop inside of the pot, and turn on the heat. Remove any foam that appear from this process.
  4. After parboiling the pork chop, rinse it with clean water. Add some oil to your pan and fry the pork chop until it appear to be a little yellow
  5. Add in dried bay leaf, Chinese cinnamon, star anise, soy sauce and some oyster sauce
  6. Pour all the coke into the pot. Then add enough water until it have cover the pork chop, and cook for 30 minutes.
  7. Season the way you like, then add the rice cakes in for 10 minutes
  8. Sparkle some sesame on top and enjoy!
